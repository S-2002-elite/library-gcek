ABOUT LIBRARY

The Library of Government College of Engineering Kalahandi is named as "Central Library", established in the year 2009 to foster literacy of all kinds. For the last 12 years, the library is providing academic services to Under-Graduate/Post-Graduate students, research scholars, faculty members and staff members of various departments and performing the role of being a lifeline of the institute. This Bookhouse of GCEK has around 16,000 books of various dep artments of the Technical Field, which includes Electrical Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Besides this, the Bookhouse also has a rich collection of books in the area of humanities & Basic Science. While a part of it also consists of approximately 1600 Journals & Magazines, daily newspapers, various references & reading room books. Read more
